FANS

Electric fans are devices that circulate air to create a cooling effect, commonly used in homes, offices, and other indoor spaces. Operating on the principle of convection, they work by moving air across the skin, facilitating the evaporation of sweat and helping to dissipate body heat, thus providing relief during hot weather or in stuffy environments. Electric fans come in various types, including ceiling fans, desk fans, floor fans, and tower fans, each designed for specific purposes and spaces. They are powered by electricity and typically feature adjustable settings for speed and oscillation to customize airflow according to individual preferences.
